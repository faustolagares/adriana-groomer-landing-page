Why it matters

Grooming is more than looks

Healthy skin & coat

Regular bathing and brushing prevent matting, tangles, and the skin issues they cause.

Comfortable paws

Trimmed nails and tidy paw pads protect your dog’s posture, balance, and joints.

Clean ears

Clearing buildup helps prevent the infections that sneak up on so many dogs.

Fresher breath

Teeth cleaning catches dental trouble early and keeps that puppy breath in check.

Before we meet

A few things I ask

  1. 1

    Book ahead

    I take one dog at a time, so appointments fill up. Reach out early to find a slot that works for you.

  2. 2

    Keep vaccines current

    Please have your dog’s vaccinations up to date for everyone’s safety. Let me know if you have any questions.

  3. 3

    Tell me about your dog

    Share any health, skin, or behavior needs beforehand so I can make the visit as calm and safe as possible.

  4. 4

    Give notice to cancel

    Life happens, so just let me know as early as you can. Same-day cancellations may be subject to a fee.
